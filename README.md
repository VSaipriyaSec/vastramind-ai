 VastraMind AI
The AI Brain Behind Every Fashion Business

VastraMind AI is an AI-powered Business Operating System designed specifically for fashion retailers, boutiques, textile stores, and clothing brands.

🚀 Vision

To transform the fashion retail industry with Artificial Intelligence by integrating billing, inventory management, customer relationship management (CRM), analytics, automation, and AI-powered business intelligence into one intelligent platform.

🎯 Features (Planned)

- 🧾 Smart Billing System
- 📦 Inventory Management
- 👥 Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- 💳 Multi-Payment Support
- 📱 WhatsApp Invoice & Notifications
- 🎁 Loyalty & Rewards
- 📊 Business Analytics Dashboard
- 🤖 AI Business Assistant
- 📈 Sales & Demand Prediction
- 👁️ Computer Vision for Inventory
- 🎬 AI Marketing Content Generator
- 💡 AI Fashion Trend Prediction
